Keep Your Heart Healthy & Strong

Simple lifestyle tips backed by healthcare professionals.

About the Heart ‚ù§

A healthy heart is a strong and efficient pump that circulates blood smoothly throughout the body, delivering oxygen and nutrients to all organs and tissues. It has flexible and clean arteries that allow blood to flow freely without blockages. Maintaining a healthy heart reduces the risk of serious conditions like heart attacks, strokes, and heart failure. Key factors include good diet, regular physical activity, managing stress, avoiding harmful habits like smoking, and monitoring heart health through regular medical checkups. A healthy heart also supports stamina, energy levels, and overall well-being, enabling an active and fulfilling life.. A healthy heart is essential for maintaining overall well-being and preventing cardiovascular diseases.

Top 5 Heart Health Tips

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Reduce sugar, salt, and saturated fats.

2. Exercise Regularly

At least 30 minutes of moderate exercise like walking, cycling, or yoga can improve heart health.

3. Manage Stress

Meditation, breathing exercises, and adequate rest help lower stress and protect your heart.

4. Avoid Smoking

Smoking increases the risk of heart diseases. Quitting smoking is one of the best steps for heart health.

5. Regular Checkups

Monitor blood pressure, cholesterol, and heart health with regular checkups for early detection.
